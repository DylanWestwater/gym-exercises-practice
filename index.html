Muscle Areas Targeted

Chest Back Shoulders Arms Core Legs

Chest

    Benchpress

    The bench press is a conventional movement that involves lying flat on top of a gym bench, lowering a bar down to the chest and pushing it back up until the arms fully extend.

    Incline benchpress

    the incline benchpress is a very similar movement to the benchpress, the difference being that the bench you were lying on is incline by roughly 45 degrees.

    Pectoral fly's

    The pectoral fly is a movement that involves lying on a bench, holding dumbells stright up in the air and bringing your arms down towards your side until your elbows are inline with th etop of your chest.
